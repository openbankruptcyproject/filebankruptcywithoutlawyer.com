What to Look For

  • Generates all required Official Bankruptcy Forms
  • Includes the means test calculation
  • Updates for current year exemption amounts and median income figures
  • Provides plain-language explanations of each question
  • Allows you to save, edit, and print your completed forms

Important: No software can replace legal advice. The software helps you fill out forms correctly, but it cannot tell you whether Chapter 7 or 13 is right for you, which exemptions to claim, or how to handle complex issues.
